BigCursorHighlighter

Minimal, functional, and beautiful

BigCursorHighlighter helps you find the cursor instantly on high‑resolution displays. Designed for 4K and other high‑DPI screens where the system cursor can easily disappear, the app highlights the pointer with two configurable rings that can be set very large so the area around the cursor becomes visually clear and distraction‑free.

Key features

  • Large dual‑ring highlight designed for high‑resolution displays — sizeable enough to clear the visual area around the cursor.
  • Full customization of both rings: size, color, opacity, and stroke width for both functional clarity and aesthetic expression.
  • Random color mode: automatically choose random colors while preserving configured opacity — useful for discovering pleasing color combinations.
  • Color harmony modes: auto‑generate complementary or analogous pairs for the two rings, allowing the colors to harmonize.
  • Auto‑hide: the highlight can automatically disappear after 5 seconds of cursor inactivity (configurable).
  • Lightweight, minimal UI that keeps the experience focused and unobtrusive.
  • In‑app purchase: Unlock the Custom Color Picker and choose any color you like — your support is appreciated.
